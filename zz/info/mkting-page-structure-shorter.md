# App Marketing Page Structure

# OBS
For each of the app marketing pages, only include what is relevant into the structure below. Also feel free to adapt each section

## 1. Hero
**Headline** — one line that makes the couple picture their wedding. Not a feature description.
**Subhead** — one sentence on what happens and why it's different.
**Visual** — phone mockup of the guest UI.
**CTA** — "Book this app — $2,000"

## 2. The Scene
3–5 sentences written as a wedding-day narrative. Time of day, setting, what guests do, what the couple feels. No product language — just the moment.

## 3. How It Works
Three columns: **You set it up** (content form, ~X min) · **Guests use it** (QR → phone, no download) · **You keep it** (name the specific keepsake)

## 4. What's on the Big Screen
One sentence + a mockup of the live display wall. This is what the whole room sees together.

## 5. Is This You?
3 bullets. Helps the right couple self-select. Be specific enough that the wrong couple opts out.

## 6. Book It
"Ready to make this yours?" + CTA button + "Have questions? Get in touch."

## 7. FAQ
4 questions max: no download? · setup time? · tech issues on the day? · what's customizable?
