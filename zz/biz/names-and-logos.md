## 10 Logo Concepts (SVG-safe)

Each concept is described so an LLM can render it reliably in SVG — no gradients, no complex paths, no fine detail.

---

### 1. Linked Rings + Phone
Two classic wedding rings side by side. The right ring's inner circle becomes a rounded-rect phone screen. Monochrome.
- Shapes: 2 circles (stroke only) + 1 rounded-rect inset in the right ring
- Difficulty: trivial

---

### 2. Heart Signal
A filled heart shape with three concentric wifi-arc lines radiating from the top-center point. Reads as "love broadcasting live."
- Shapes: heart path (two arcs + lines) + 3 short arcs
- Difficulty: easy

---

### 3. W Monogram with Rings
Bold capital W. The two inner peaks of the W are capped with small circles, turning the letter into two rings side by side.
- Shapes: 1 polyline (W form) + 2 small circles
- Difficulty: trivial

---

### 4. Champagne Glasses Clinking (minimal)
Two triangles (flutes) pointing up, slightly angled toward each other, stems as thin lines, a starburst dot at the clink point.
- Shapes: 2 triangles + 2 lines + 1 small starburst (6 short lines from center)
- Difficulty: easy

---

### 5. Phone + Bow
Portrait phone outline (rounded-rect). A simple ribbon bow sits centered on the screen area. Feels like a gift — which it is.
- Shapes: 1 rounded-rect + 2 curved triangles (bow loops) + 1 short vertical line (knot)
- Difficulty: easy

---

### 6. Star-Dot Ring
A ring/circle shape made entirely of evenly-spaced filled dots (12 dots, clock positions). Simple, festive, timeless.
- Shapes: 12 small circles arranged on a common radius
- Difficulty: trivial — use `transform="rotate(Ndeg)"` on each

---

### 7. Two Interlocked Hearts
Two heart outlines overlapping at center like linked rings. Clean, symmetrical, works at any size.
- Shapes: 2 heart paths (stroke only), offset horizontally by ~30%
- Difficulty: moderate (heart path) but well-known SVG formula

---

### 8. Phone with Confetti
Portrait phone outline. Three small squares/diamonds scattered inside the screen area at random angles — implies celebration happening on-screen.
- Shapes: 1 rounded-rect + 3 small `<rect>` elements with `transform="rotate"` applied
- Difficulty: trivial

---

### 9. Ring + Sparkle
A single wedding ring (circle, stroke only) with four 4-pointed star sparkles arranged around it at compass points.
- Shapes: 1 circle + 4 simple 4-pointed stars (two crossing lines or small polygon)
- Difficulty: easy

---

### 10. Block-Text Lock-Up (wordmark only)
The studio name set in two weights: first word bold, second word light, stacked or inline. A single thin horizontal rule between. No icon — confidence through simplicity.
- Shapes: pure text + 1 `<line>`
- Difficulty: trivial — fully LLM-reliable
- Best for: early-stage before visual identity is locked
