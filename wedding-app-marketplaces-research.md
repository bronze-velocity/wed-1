# Wedding App Marketplaces & Platforms — Research

**Compiled:** 2026-07-18
**Purpose:** Survey of 20 marketplaces / platforms across the wedding lifecycle (before → during → after) where wedding apps are discovered, distributed, or where couples and vendors find and list app-based services. Useful for Wepho positioning, competitive context, and distribution/listing opportunities.

> Note on "marketplace": the list below mixes three distinct types — (a) **vendor/service marketplaces** where a business like Wepho could be listed, (b) **app distribution stores**, and (c) **destination platforms** that themselves are the marketplace of wedding tools couples shop from. Each entry notes which it is.

---

## Before the Wedding — Planning & Vendor Marketplaces

### 1. The Knot Marketplace
The largest U.S. wedding vendor marketplace. Vendors pay to be listed; couples find, contact, and book pros directly. Also an all-in-one planning app (checklist, website, guest list, budget, AI vendor matching).
- Type: Vendor marketplace + planning platform
- Source: https://www.theknot.com/marketplace

### 2. WeddingWire
Sister brand to The Knot (both under The Knot Worldwide) but an independent hub. Vendor directory of 150,000+ pros plus planning tools (RSVPs, drag-and-drop seating chart).
- Type: Vendor marketplace + planning platform
- Source: https://www.theknot.com/content/how-to-find-local-wedding-vendors

### 3. Zola
All-in-one platform (website, registry, guest list, budget) with a smaller, less-crowded vendor directory — easier standout visibility for listed businesses. Adding AI thank-you and schedule drafting.
- Type: Vendor marketplace + planning platform
- Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/best-websites-for-finding-vendors

### 4. Style Me Pretty Vendor Guide
Highly curated, premium-leaning vendor directory trusted by couples seeking quality over volume — a "quality exposure" complement to the big directories.
- Type: Curated vendor marketplace
- Source: https://www.stylemepretty.com/2025/09/26/the-best-websites-to-list-your-wedding-business-in-2025/

### 5. Joy (withjoy.com)
Free all-in-one wedding website + planning platform (guest list, RSVP, registry, vendor discovery) frequently compared alongside The Knot and Zola.
- Type: Planning platform + vendor discovery
- Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/best-free-wedding-planning-websites

### 6. Folia Planner
Visual, collaborative all-in-one planning app (budgets, guest lists, RSVPs, vendors, to-dos). Generous free tier, paid from ~$11.99/mo.
- Type: Planning platform
- Source: https://www.100layercake.com/2026/05/12/wedding-planning-apps-websites/

### 7. WedSites
Wedding website + planning app for couples, positioned among the best planning websites/apps for 2026.
- Type: Planning platform
- Source: https://blog.wedsites.com/8-of-the-best-wedding-planning-websites-and-apps-for-couples-in-2026/

## During the Wedding — Guest Engagement, Games & Live Sharing

### 8. My Wedding Trivia
Live trivia + betting games + photo wall for guests, no app download. Live big-screen MC mode or at-leisure QR-per-table mode. Closest direct analog to Wepho's interactive-guest concept.
- Type: Interactive guest-app platform
- Source: https://myweddingtrivia.com/

### 9. Kahoot (Wedding Games)
General live quiz platform with a dedicated wedding-games use case. Guests join via QR / game PIN at kahoot.it — no downloads. Fills cocktail-hour / dinner downtime.
- Type: Interactive guest-app platform
- Source: https://kahoot.com/home/wedding-games/

### 10. Jotform Trivia Maker
Build a shareable trivia game guests play on their phones via link or QR. DIY, form-builder-based.
- Type: DIY interactive game builder
- Source: https://www.jotform.com/blog/wedding-trivia-questions/

### 11. Guestpix
QR-code guest photo sharing with a live slideshow feature used during receptions to spark participation on a projector/big screen.
- Type: Live photo sharing + slideshow
- Source: https://guestpix.com/weddings/

### 12. Kululu
Free QR-code wedding photo-sharing app; guests scan to contribute, no downloads. Real-time collection during the event.
- Type: Live photo sharing
- Source: https://www.kululu.com/wedding-photo-sharing-app

### 13. Pix Wedding (pix.wedding)
Purpose-built guest photo sharing — scan a QR, shoot on any device, images auto-upload to a shared gallery with no guest app download.
- Type: Live photo sharing
- Source: https://www.pix.wedding/best-wedding-planning-apps

### 14. Revel.cam
Dedicated guest photo plan recommended as the "add-on" to an all-in-one planner so memories don't scatter across texts and albums.
- Type: Live photo sharing
- Source: https://revel.cam/blog/best-wedding-planning-apps-in-2026-what-to-use-and-skip

### 15. Wedibox
Digital guestbook + media-sharing: QR upload of photos, videos, audio messages, and written notes; unlimited hi-res; real-time live slideshow.
- Type: Live sharing + digital guestbook
- Source: https://eventpics.net/en/blog/digital-wedding-guestbook.html

## After the Wedding — Galleries, Guestbooks & Keepsakes

### 16. GuestCam
Private post-event gallery collecting guest photos, videos, and voice messages via QR/link; couples view, organize, display, and download afterward.
- Type: Digital guestbook / post-event gallery
- Source: https://guestcam.co/blog/top-digital-wedding-guestbooks

### 17. Once (once.film)
Premium private "shared disposable camera" — guests join via QR, album reveals after the event; AI organizes into a printable timeline photo book.
- Type: Post-event gallery + photo book
- Source: https://once.film/blog/best-wedding-apps-2026

### 18. Google Photos
General consumer platform used as a wedding fallback: auto-backup, shared albums, QR-based album sharing for collecting and distributing after the day.
- Type: Consumer photo platform
- Source: https://www.wedpicsqr.com/blog/apps-for-weddings

### 19. Camdeed
QR-code guest photo sharing + digital guestbook, spanning live capture and post-event collection.
- Type: Photo sharing + digital guestbook
- Source: https://camdeed.com/weddings

### 20. Journi
Photo-book creation for weddings — guest books, parent albums, and keepsake print products assembled after the celebration.
- Type: Post-event photo book / keepsake
- Source: https://www.journiapp.com/e/wpbi/

## Takeaways for Wepho

- The **during-the-wedding interactive** space (My Wedding Trivia, Kahoot, live-slideshow photo apps) is Wepho's closest competitive set — all lean heavily on the "no download, scan a QR" pattern Wepho also uses.
- Most competitors are **self-serve, generic templates**. Wepho's differentiation (custom, couple-specific $2K builds) reinforces the brand's core subtext: *generic tools don't know who you are.*
- **Vendor marketplaces** (The Knot, WeddingWire, Zola, Style Me Pretty) are the realistic **listing/distribution channels** for a service business like Wepho, not the app stores.

## Agencies / Companies That Produce Custom Wedding Apps (Competitor Scan)

**Key finding first:** the vendor marketplaces above (The Knot, WeddingWire, Zola) do **not** have a "custom wedding app" or "interactive guest experience" category. WeddingWire runs ~25 fixed vendor categories (planners, florists, DJs, entertainment, etc.) — a Wepho-style builder can only shoehorn into "Entertainment" or "Wedding Websites." So these agencies are **not discoverable inside the marketplaces**; they surface through general web search. This is itself a positioning insight: Wepho is naming a category the marketplaces don't yet have, but couples also aren't shopping the marketplaces for it.

The players sort into three tiers by delivery model:

### Tier 1 — Custom-branded app services (closest to Wepho)
- **Bravo Studio** — "custom-branded app for your wedding guests," design-first, iOS/Android, interactive features for a "wow-factor." Nearest positioning match, but app-store native rather than a no-download web app. — https://www.bravostudio.app/custom-branded-wedding-app
- **Audiorista** — no-code platform to build **white-label**, branded wedding-planner apps; explicitly pitched at agencies producing a unique app per wedding. — https://www.audiorista.com/app-builder-tool-for/wedding-planning-app
- **DreamCo Design** — design/dev agency building custom iOS/Android wedding & event apps for businesses. — https://www.dreamcodesign.com/weddingevent-app-development.php
- **Sapphire Solutions** — custom wedding-planner app development company (dev shop). — https://www.sapphiresolutions.net/wedding-planner-app-development-services

### Tier 2 — DIY / templated app builders (self-serve, not agencies)
- **Appy Couple** — personalized website + coordinating guest app, 500+ designs, digital guestbook, virtual toasts, collaborative playlist. Biggest incumbent in "couple-branded guest app." — https://www.appycouple.com/
- **Appy Pie** — no-code wedding app builder; own-branding only on the top plan. — https://www.appypie.com/wedding-app-builder
- **Jotform Wedding App Builder** — no-code app with RSVP, countdown, venue map. — https://www.jotform.com/wedding-app-builder/
- **Guidebook** — drag-and-drop builder used to assemble free wedding apps. — https://www.guidebook.com/post/monika-made-a-free-wedding-app
- **Glide** — no-code platform + agency partners for custom wedding-service apps in ~4 weeks. — https://www.glideapps.com/solutions/wedding-planning-services/project-management-software

### Tier 3 — Live interactive-experience / entertainment hosts (service, not software)
- **My Wedding Trivia** — self-serve live trivia + prop bets + photo wall (also in the main list). — https://myweddingtrivia.com/
- **Trivia Mafia (Events)** — hired hosts running customized interactive trivia at private events incl. weddings. — https://www.triviamafia.com/events
- **Trivia CA** — pub-quiz company booked to host reception trivia. — https://playtriviaca.com/hire-us-book-a-show
- **Here's Your Host** — custom game shows built per group, incl. weddings. — https://heresyourhost.com/privatevirtual
- **TrivWorks** — custom interactive entertainment with professional emcees for private events. — https://trivworks.com/

### Read-through for Wepho
- **No competitor occupies Wepho's exact slot:** bespoke, per-couple, no-download web app, ~$2K, one night. Tier 1 is app-store native (Bravo) or white-label tooling (Audiorista); Tier 2 is generic templates; Tier 3 is human hosts.
- The gap is real, but so is the **discovery problem** — none of these live where couples shop (the marketplaces), which reinforces that Wepho's growth channel is likely **SEO/content + planner referrals**, not marketplace listings.
